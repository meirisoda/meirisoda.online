notes

Notes is not a blog. It is titled notes as a way to represent my digital notebook.

I do not separate my journals, diaries, notebooks or commonplace books, and instead prefer to have them all in one place. While there are benefits to separating them out (seen in the recent surge in popularity of commonplace books), I've found that letting myself place everything in one place reduced the barrier to entry and the feeling of permanence and finality that would come with a diary.

#7b7b7b

As such, my notebook becomes an extension of myself. It may contain writings surrounding world-centered topics, as well as person-centered topics from various perspectives.

It also contains various journal/blog posts archived from previous iterations of my website (details here). I have yet to compile some of my new writings, but they will be posted on this page, or on a subdirectory.
