meirisoda.github.io was my first website. There are many iterations, 4 of which are live below:

meirisoda.github.io/1

The first version of my website, and stylistically closest to the current site. As I was learning HTML, I didn't know the capabilities of CSS, and as such, the code is very messy (it is still messy).


meirisoda.github.io/2

Personally, my least favorite version. Lacks a cohesive body and unifying idea, and was a shallow attempt at creating a minimalist and "professional" website, ending up not representing me or my values at all.


meirisoda.github.io/3 — This version of the website served as an experiment - both in what I could achieve from a website, but also for my identity.

meirisoda.github.io/4 — Built off /3, this version is my personal favorite and what I think looks the most polished.
