infinition

My first visual novel/interactive artwork; written and coded on twine.

This was created for a class assignment in 2021. While the writing isn't very good, nor is there a very clear storyline, I believe that this is a piece that encapsulates the art I want to create and the feelings I want to convey in all of its imperfections.

Play it on itch.io or on meirisoda.github.io.

The piece was largely inspired by Infinition by Quadrant (2004).
